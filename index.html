ZALANDO NEW DESIGN SYSTEM
Design System Activation OOH Rollout Print Implementation

The Challenge

Implementing new visual elements that strengthen recognition, enhance emotional resonance, and perform at large scale.

Zalando is Europe's leading online platform for fashion and lifestyle. In 2020, a new Design System launched to strengthen emotional connection and express its inclusive, bold, and playful personality more consistently across touchpoints.


As Senior Print Designer, I helped develop key core brand elements and translated them into impactful print and Out-of-Home executions, bringing the system to life in physical spaces before it rolled out across wider communications.

The Strategy

The new Design System was built around three core components: a dual-logo system, a refined typographic pairing of Helvetica Now and Tiempos, and a flexible grid framework.


My role focused on ensuring these elements worked cohesively across both large-scale and standard OOH formats, balancing expression with clarity and consistency before extending into paid advertising and social media.


The Outcome

A cohesive and recognisable visual system that strengthened Zalando's emotional positioning and established a scalable framework for consistent communication across all channels.

PR PACKAGING REDESIGN

The Challenge

Redesign Zalando's influencer PR packaging to create a premium, fashion-led unboxing experience inspiring and highly shareable, yet subtly branded.

Concept Packaging System Print Execution

The Challenge

Redesign Zalando's influencer PR packaging to create a premium, fashion-led unboxing experience — inspiring and highly shareable, yet subtly branded.

The goal was to move away from heavy logo usage toward a more considered, editorial feel that would feel at home in the hands of fashion influencers.

The Strategy

We elevated Zalando's iconic orange by developing a refined tonal spectrum — from soft peach to deep burnt orange — intentionally stepping outside strict brand guidelines to achieve a more sophisticated feel. Colour became the brand expression.


The Outcome

A system of four core elements: box, paper bag, envelope and card. Each designed to create a seamless reveal — a curated, camera-ready unboxing journey optimised for social media storytelling.

C&A BRAND IDENTITY
Brand Evolution Visual System Development Cross-channel Guidelines

The Challenge

Reinventing a 180-year heritage brand for a modern audience — under significant budget constraints, with stronger recognition beyond the logo.

C&A is a heritage fashion brand with over 180 years of history. I joined the Brand Design team at a pivotal moment of transformation, contributing to a shift in how the brand prepared for the future.


Significant budget cuts forced a major strategic rethink. Costly location shoots were no longer viable. We had to reinvent the brand while maintaining emotional connection and honouring the legacy.

The Strategy

We shifted from high-cost shoots to a distinctive visual system built on strong colour consistency, diverse casting, simplified design, and clear brand rules across every touchpoint.


I was the sole designer responsible for the Brand Guidelines, building and continuously evolving the system over several years. These guidelines became the foundation for all internal teams and external agencies.


The Outcome

A cohesive, cost-conscious brand system that modernised C&A while preserving its heritage, enabling emotional storytelling without heavy production budgets and creating long-term consistency across all channels.

ALBUM ARTWORK
Art Direction Visual Identity Concept Development

The Brief

Developing a visual identity for Nico Leivo — simple, honest, and dreamy.

Nico Leivo is an up-and-coming Argentine-born, Berlin-based musician creating introspective alternative-pop. Nico shared early tracks along with a few defining words which became the guiding principles for the visual direction.

The Outcome

After exploring a range of visual directions, we landed on the concept of a faceless figure holding two contrasting expressions — happy and sad. This became a central metaphor, capturing the emotional duality of life, love and friendship explored throughout the album.
